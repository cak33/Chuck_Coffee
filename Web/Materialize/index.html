Easy Connectivity

The UBrew coffee pot connects to your homes Wi-Fi the same way your smart phone or computer does. Once connected on your homes Wi-Fi network, simply open the application and control your UBrew coffee pot. A simple and effective interface allows you to set up schedules for when to brew coffee as well as to check the levels of the grounds and water.
