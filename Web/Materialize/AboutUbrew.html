About UBrew

UBrew is the first "one-stop-shop" for the modern day coffee drinker. Feel like pressing the alarm for 5 extra minutes? No problem. Start brewing the desired quantity of coffee from your smart phone. Better yet, forget to set up the timer for your brew schedule? No problem. Set a schedule depending on what you have going on right from your smart phone. Make a pot of coffee and forget that you have no more left? No problem. U Brew sends you push notifications based upon analytics from our UBrew IoT machine, so you can place an order for a fresh bag to be delivered so you will never run out. UBrew IoT fosters both the supply and brewing processes, all while maintaining top quality and freshness through our unique coffee storage unit within the UBrew machine.

The UBrew Mission

Here at UBrew, we realize how pivitol a cup of coffee can be. We also realize how powerful technology can be. Having a passion to enhance and add value to society, UBrew is committed to enhancing the coffee process by making it more efficient for the user. UBrew is an essential for the working man or woman, the stay-at-home parent, the college student, and anyone who would like to maximize the use of his or her time. Admittedly, here at UBrew, we are slight coffee snobs and have that one favorite brand where it makes all the difference. For that reason, we use the design and the analytics derived from the UBrew machine to our advantage, assuring that the consumer will always have a fresh cup of coffee when brewed, and that he or she can purchase his or her favorite type or even try new types from around the country, within the touch of a button.
